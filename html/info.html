Caleb is a cinematographer currently living and working in Tāmaki Makaurau. His work focuses on narrative and a considered naturalistic approach. Having recently moved from Pōneke, Wellington, where he worked primarily on feature productions, he is now prioritising shooting smaller, conceptually led, projects.

Caleb has an honest approach to creating, and is interested in making work that is authentic and timeless. If that sounds like you, get in touch.
